Bug Report

Description: The Win - Loss counter to keep track of how many times a player has won or lost a match counts all results no matter the outcome of the match.

Steps to Reproduce: Select 2 bots with high level attacks in order to increase odds of victory and click the duel button. A message will appear saying whether the player has won or lost.

Expected Result: If the player loses the number of losses should increase by one. If the player wins the number of wins should increase by one.

Actual Result: The number of losses increses by one regardless of the outcome of the duel while the number of wins remains at 0.
